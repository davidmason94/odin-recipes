Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Minced Beef
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Pasta
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the Minced Beef
  2. Add the Tomatoes
  3. Put the ingredients into a glass baking tray with Pasta laid within it
  4. Cover the contents and cook in the oven for 1 hour on 180 degrees C
  5. Cover it in cheese and you're good to go
