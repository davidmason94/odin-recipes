Chili Con Carne

Ingredients

  • Minced Beef
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Kidney Beans
  • Rice
  • Red Chili

Steps

  1. Cook the Minced Beef and kidney beans
  2. Add the Canned Tomatoes
  3. Break up the tomatoes, add the chili and proceed to simmer for 1 hour
  4. Wash the rice and cook for 15 minutes
